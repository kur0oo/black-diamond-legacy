Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Black Diamond Legacy, LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information you provide when using our website or communicating with us.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you submit a property inquiry, including:

  • Property address and location details
  • Parcel size, zoning, and condition information
  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Any additional context you choose to share about your property or situation

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Evaluate your property and provide a cash offer
  • Communicate with you regarding your inquiry
  • Send SMS text messages if you have opted in to receive them (see SMS Consent below)
  • Respond to your questions or requests
  • Improve our services and website

SMS Text Messages

If you opt in to receive SMS text messages, we will only use your phone number to communicate with you regarding your property inquiry. Message frequency varies. Standard msg & data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS messages at any time, reply STOP to any message you receive. You may also contact us directly to request removal from our messaging list.

We Do Not Sell Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is used solely for communication related to your property inquiry.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. If you request that we stop communicating with you, we will remove your information from our active communications list.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve user experience. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP

SMS Consent Disclosure

When you submit a property inquiry and opt in to SMS messaging, you consent to receive text messages from Black Diamond Legacy, LLC at the phone number you provide. Your consent is:

  • Affirmative and explicit (checkbox-based, not implied)
  • Timestamped at the time of submission
  • Stored with your contact record
  • Voluntary and revocable at any time by replying STOP
